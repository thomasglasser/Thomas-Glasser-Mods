Though these are some things I have done in the past, I am always open to learning new things. If you have a request not listed here, feel free to contact me.

Things I Can Do
Element Details Examples
Items Items are objects that can be picked up and placed in the player's inventory. They can be used to craft other items, interact with the world, or place blocks.
Blocks Blocks are objects that can be placed in the world. They can be used to build structures, create barriers, or provide light. They may also have GUIs or contain items.
Entities Entities are objects that can move and interact with the world. They can be hostile or friendly, passive or aggressive, and may have AI. All entities I create will be powered by the new Brain system using SmartBrainLib, allowing advanced behaviors.
Structures Structures are buildings or other placements of blocks that generate in the world. They can be natural, like trees or lakes, or buildings like villages, mansions, or dungeons. Structures are also able to use the Jigsaw system for randomization and large builds.
